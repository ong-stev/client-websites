Elle's Cafe

San Jose, California

Learn More

We strive to create an inviting and enjoyable cafe experience

Hamilton and Elle Marley established this cafe in 2014. The idea of starting a cafe started when they weren't able to find a studying/hangout place in their neighborhood that they really liked. Elle's Cafe became a passion project to create their ideal cafe experience.

Equipped with many tables for both solo and group work with large windows to allow in natural light makes our cafe a popular location for both students and working adults. There might even be days where you can catch us catching up on paperwork at the cafe!
